Important Reminders

Don't forget these essential tasks and items for our cruise celebration

Before November 12, 2025
Deadline

Final Payment Due

Ensure your final cruise payment is submitted by midnight on November 11, 2025.

Essential Documents
Required

Travel Documents

Check that your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond the cruise date. Print your cruise tickets and boarding pass. Keep digital and physical copies of all important documents.

Passport Requirements
Required

Travel Documents

British passport holders require an ESTA, and Nigerian passport holders require an American visa.

Extra Money
Required

Travel Expense

Please remember to hold extra money if you want to go on excursions, go shopping, or to pay for anything that isn't included in the cruise package.

Travel to and from the Ship
Required

Extra Travel Costs

Please have cash on hand to pay for transportation, which you can claim as a $100 voucher once onboard the ship

Onboard WiFi
Not essential

WiFi

You can purchase the WiFi in advance on their website.

Packing Essentials
Don't Forget

What to Pack

Formal attire for evening meals, comfortable walking shoes for shore excursions, sunscreen and swimwear, any necessary medications, and your camera to capture special moments!

Before Departure
Week Before

Pre-Cruise Checklist

Notify your bank of travel dates to avoid card issues. Set up an out-of-office email reply. Arrange pet care or house sitting if needed. Check weather forecasts for destinations.

Health & Safety
Important

Health Preparations

Check if any vaccinations are required for ports of call. Pack a basic first aid kit and any prescription medications with extra supplies. Consider travel insurance for peace of mind.

Day Before Departure
Final Check

Last-Minute Items

Charge all electronic devices and pack chargers. Exchange some currency if needed. Review the cruise itinerary one final time. Get a good night's sleep before the big day!
